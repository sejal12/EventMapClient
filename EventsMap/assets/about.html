IIT Guwahati Map Project

The Map project, with an interactive and engaging interface, aims to provide a virtual tour of the campus.

This is a "one of its kind" project ever undertaken by students by their own initiative with support from the administration.
Hope this sparks new hope among students and administration alike to take up more such projects which benefit the
larger campus fraternity.
